About me

Hello there! I am Khoa, an aspiring game developer that has also dipped my toes in some other areas of programming with at least something to show. I am rather proficient in English, to the point where I would say more so than my Vietnamese. Having graduated with a Bachelor degree in Computer Science at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology in late 2023 with only a couple of months of experience working professionally, I am hungry to expand my knowledge and look to constantly challenge myself mentally through coding to question my perception of realities and perhaps my very own existence, also I would love to be generating income too :D.

Custom Music Player 3

Outside of all of those, I am proficient in Python, or at least proficient enough to make a working desktop application using the ever popular Qt GUI library. Its purpose is to play audio, with a twist of being capable of looping around user-defined loop points to achieve what is referred to as seamless looping, often seen in game soundtracks. Or you can make jokey loops too if you want, like "X but it never starts". Also 3 because this is my 3rd execution of this idea.

The project can be found here, compilation instructions included.
