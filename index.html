Totally Not A Touhou Ripoff - Extra


Instructions

Use WASD or arrow keys to move.

Hold Shift to focus and slow down.

Press Space to start/pause/resume/restart game.

Press X to bomb and clear the screen of bullets. For each bullet cleared this way, you also gain points.

Your goal is to survive for 60 seconds and earn as many points as possible by grazing (getting close to bullets without getting hit). Unused bombs are also converted into points.
